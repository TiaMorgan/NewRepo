Peach Pie

Price: $18.95

Description

Bethany received the receipe for this delicious peach pie from her mother. At the age of 15, she was already baking this all-American traditional peach pie at her home. As the recipe goes a long way back, it contains the most typical ingredients used for so many years to make this classic even today one of our most popular pies.

This pie is availabe every day and comes in 2 sizes. You can serve it cold or warm it for 4 minutes in the microwave to give it an extra touch.Your house will smell just like Bethany's Shop!

This recipe has been in our family for generations. We hope you enjoy this pie as we do - Bethany

How did we make this pie

  1. First, we combine sugar, flour and spices. We toss the peaches with some lemon juice and add sugar.

  2. We then create the bottom crust and fill that with peach mixture. We put some butter on that. We then roll remaining crust to fit top of pie.

  3. Then we beat the eggs white until we have a foamy mix. That is then brushed over the crust. We sprinkle some extra sugar.

  4. Then the pie goes into the oven at 360Â° for 35 minutes. When ready, we let it cool on a wire rack.

  5. If something goes wrong, we start all over again!
